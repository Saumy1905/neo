About PYQFort

A collection of PYQs (Previous Year Questions) for various colleges and universities.

Aim

PYQFort is a free open source project aims to serve PYQs to B.Tech. colleges all over nation.

What We Offer

  • A comprehensive collection of PDFs organized by colleges -> branch -> sem -> subject -> pdf
  • Online PDF viewing without the need for additional software
  • Easy download options for offline access
  • Simple navigation and search capabilities
  • Regular updates with new PYQs
